Our History

We establish our farm in the beautiful meadows of Queesland, Australia in the late 60's. Since then we are producing standard quality breeds like Mustang, Andalusian, Percheron and Holsteiner for our customers. We took these breed from different parts of the world and start growing them and now we are well-known all over Australia

Our horses are trained and well-equiped to provide a wonderful user-experience to the riders. Mentors tame horses carefully for the ease of their user.
